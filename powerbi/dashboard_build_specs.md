# Dashboard Build Specs

This file converts the project brief into a PBIX authoring checklist.

## 01 Executive Market Overview

Pages:

1. Market Snapshot
2. Sector Performance
3. YoY Growth Tracker

Must include:

- cards for total companies, average ROE, excellent-health count, weak/poor count
- sector distribution
- health label distribution
- top 10 by ROE
- bottom 10 by growth
- sector average OPM matrix
- revenue and net profit trends

## 02 Company Deep Dive

Pages:

1. Financial Summary
2. Balance Sheet Health
3. Cash Flow Analysis
4. Growth and Returns Analysis

Must include:

- company selector on every page
- health score gauge
- revenue versus profit combo
- OPM trend
- EPS trend
- debt to equity trend
- asset composition
- free cash flow trend
- cash conversion ratio
- dividend history

## 03 Sector Comparison Analyzer

Pages:

1. Sector vs Sector
2. Companies Within a Sector
3. Sector Trends Over Time

Must include:

- multi-sector comparison
- within-sector rankings
- leverage and profitability trend lines
- sector scorecard matrix

## 04 Financial Health Scorecard

Pages:

1. Health Score Leaderboard
2. Scorecard Breakdown

Must include:

- score distribution
- weak/poor attention table
- dimension-level score radar
- peer comparison
- pros and cons lists

## 05 Growth and Valuation Analytics

Pages:

1. Revenue and Profit Growth
2. Margin Evolution
3. EPS and Earnings Quality

Must include:

- growth scatter
- consistent growers table
- OPM heat map
- interest coverage trend
- EPS versus cash conversion

## 06 Debt and Leverage Monitor

Pages:

1. Leverage Snapshot
2. Debt Trajectory

Must include:

- D/E heat map
- borrowings trend
- D/E versus interest coverage scatter
- debt-free company list
- financing activity as debt signal

## 07 Dividend and Shareholder Returns

Pages:

1. Dividend Analysis
2. Shareholder Value

Must include:

- payout ranking
- consistent dividend payers
- payout ratio versus EPS growth
- EPS compounding
- ROE comparison
- stock price CAGR table

## Authoring workflow recommendation

1. Create one base PBIX with all tables, relationships, sort settings, and shared measures.
2. Save seven copies from that base file.
3. Build visuals per file using this spec.
4. Standardize slicer placement, tooltip styling, and page navigation across the suite.
